My education

ARENA MULTIMEDIA

Arena Multimedia is one of the renowned training systems specializing in multimedia arts in Vietnam and worldwide. It provides training in skills such as graphic design, web design, digital filmmaking, film post-production, 3D animation, and other fields related to multimedia arts.

TON DUC THANG UNIVERSITY

Ton Duc Thang University (TDTU) is one of the leading universities in Vietnam, renowned for its high-quality education, modern facilities, and international learning environment. Established in 1997, TDTU has quickly established its position within the higher education system of Vietnam and the region.

I began my educational journey at Bui Thi Xuan High School, where I was not only equipped with foundational knowledge but also learned how to shape my goals for the future. After that, I continued to pursue my passion at Ton Duc Thang University (TDTU), an educational environment with an international orientation, where I gradually developed my expertise and gained experience. 

Currently, in addition to continuing my studies at TDTU to deepen my knowledge in my major, I am also enrolled at Arena Multimedia to expand my skills in the field of multimedia arts. This combination not only helps me improve myself but also provides a multifaceted perspective, preparing me for the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.

Experience

I have gained valuable experience through roles such as event organizer, content creator, and photographer. In event organizing, I have been involved in key tasks like planning, managing schedules, and coordinating teams to ensure everything runs smoothly. This role helped me sharpen my organizational skills while also improving my ability to problem-solve and handle pressure in critical situations. 

As a content creator, I worked on conceptualizing ideas, creating engaging content, and developing social media strategies to resonate with the target audience. This required me to stay updated with trends and master digital tools to produce high-quality work. 

Additionally, I have experience as a photographer for personal projects and events. This role allowed me to refine my creativity, understand lighting, framing, and storytelling through each shot. Each area has contributed significantly to my growth, making me more adaptable and ready to face new challenges in my professional journey.
